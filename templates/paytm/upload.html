Export a Transaction Report from the Paytm for Business app and upload it here. Duplicate transactions are automatically skipped.

How to export for a full operational day

Paytm's end date is exclusive. To cover operational date X (06:00 X → 05:59 X+1), select:

  • From: X (the operational date)
  • To: X+2 (two days after)

Example: for Apr 18 operational day → select Apr 18 to Apr 20.

What gets imported?

  • Only ACQUIRING + SUCCESS rows are kept
  • UPI — all QR / app payments (including UPI credit card)
  • Card — physical card terminal (EDC) transactions
  • Each transaction is assigned to its operational day (06:00 – 05:59)
